1) To stop ACPI BIOS errors, add "libata.noacpi=1"   in /etc/default/grub for the option 
   GRUB_CMDLINE_LINUX_DEFAULT

> sudo su
> gedit /etc/default/grub
> GRUB_CMDLINE_LINUX_DEFAULT="quiet splash libata.noacpi=1"
> update-grub
